  • iLL Psychz

Biography

  • Basically started listening to rap music at the age of 10 whilst living in England, not because I wanted to but because my older brother did. Moved back home to Ireland when I was 12. At around 13 I started to take a keener interest in it and at 14 I began writing my own material just for fun. At 16 myself and a mate from my local town formed a group known as 'Double Dice' and we released a mixtape together. Performed at local talent shows and more until I was 19 and the group disbanded. 6 months after I got back into the booth with a re-newed passion to create music that actually meant something to me or at least carried some substance. It's a style I've chosen to run with ever since. Released my first ever solo project "Ill Psychz" in April 2011. After which I shot two videos to tracks off it. Revolutionary S.O.M and Armour. The mixtape and Revolutionary S.O.M even got a very supporting review in Hot Press which was amazing. Recently shot a video to "Weetabix (Wake Up!)" which is a track off my upcoming "Fitting In Is Overrated" project and even got an article in Irelands very own Rap Ireland which really meant a lot to me. Plans are in place for one more video for 2011. I've saved the most ambitious till last in my opinion.

  • Yellowbridge

Biography

  • Yellowbridge are an indie/pop collaboration made up of Kyle Hyunsung Kim and Ciara Donnelly. The producer/ singer songwriter have been together for a mere few months yet have developed their own sound based on influences stemming from past industry experience. The group hope to bring together the unlikely pairing that is Yellowbridge and create an album that is versatile, surprising and as pleasant as the group themselves (they swear)!
